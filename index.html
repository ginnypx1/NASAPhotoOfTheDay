NASA Photo of the Day

"NASA Photo of the Day" downloads and displays NASA's Astronomy Photo of the Day, and allows the user to read the information about the photo as well as save and share the photo.

"Nasa Photo of the Day" is an app developed to practice networking and API calls, JSON, MVC structure and Unit Tests.

The Astronomy Photo of the Day is provided by NASA

Screenshots are provided by App Launchpad.

To download "NASA Photo of the Day", visit my Github repository at: NASA Photo of the Day.
